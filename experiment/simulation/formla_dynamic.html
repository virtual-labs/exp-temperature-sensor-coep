Formula:
τ=(x/k)*ρ*L*s
Where,
x is thickness(m),
k is thermal conductivity of material ( W/m-k),
ρ is density of material (kg/m3),
s = Specific heat capacity of the material (J/Kg- oc),
L = length of the element (m). For bare element the Thickness:1.25mm Length:100mm
For Time constant for Withsheath the total time constant is

τ_sheath=τ_bareelement+τ_air+τ_sheath

For air Thickness:0.2mm Length: 15mm
For sheath consider selected thickness and Length:15mm

For Time constant for thermowell the total time constant is

τ_thermowell=τ_sheath+τ_filling material+τ_thermowell

For filling material Thickness:2.5mm and Length:15mm
For thermowell consider selected thickness and Length:15mm
